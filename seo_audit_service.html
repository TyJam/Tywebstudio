Components of a Full-Site SEO Audit

Technical SEO for Restaurants

With Google driving 92% of all search engine activity, optimizing your restaurant’s website to meet their latest algorithmic and technical standards is crucial. At TyWebStudio, our technical SEO audits are meticulously crafted to align with these advancements, ensuring your site achieves top performance.

When Google rolled out major updates like Core Web Vitals, we immediately expanded our audit scope to include essential factors such as:

  • Largest Contentful Paint (LCP): Measuring page load performance.
  • First Input Delay (FID): Evaluating user interaction speeds.
  • Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS): Ensuring visual stability for a seamless experience.

What We Assess in Our Technical SEO Audits:

  • Core Web Vitals: Speed, navigation, and mobile-friendliness.
  • Broken Internal Links: Fixing navigation gaps for a smoother user journey.
  • 301 Redirects & 4XX Status Codes: Addressing missing or broken pages (e.g., 404 errors).
  • L Canonicalization: Preventing duplicate content issues.
  • Site Crawl Errors: Ensuring your site is indexed correctly.
  • HTML/CSS Code Review: Optimizing technical structures for better performance.
  • Internal Link Structure: Enhancing site navigation and SEO value.

On-Page SEO: Fine-Tuning Every Page for Peak Performance

At TyWebStudio, we understand the importance of optimizing every detail on your restaurant's website. Our On-Page SEO audits focus on enhancing the content and structure of each page and post to align with both search engine algorithms and customer preferences.

  • Keywords and semantically-related
  • Proper Use of Headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Title Tags and Meta Descriptions
  • Local Search Results Optimization
  • Word Count Analysis
  • Alt Image Tag Optimization
  • Keyword Rankings & Traffic
  • Duplicate Content Detection

OFF-PAGE SEO

Our SEO audit delves into your off-page tactics, offering actionable recommendations to expand your reach and amplify your online presence.

  • Link building through relevant and Authoritative source
  • Email list building
  • Directory submissions up-to-date and valid
  • Blog and forum posting
