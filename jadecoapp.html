About the project

  1. JadecoApp is all about modernizing waste management in Africa. It's a user-friendly mobile app that simplifies waste collection, promotes recycling, and empowers communities to make eco-friendly choices, ultimately creating a cleaner and greener future for the continent.
  2. JadecoApp is on a mission to reduce environmental pollution and foster a culture of recycling across Africa. By offering an intuitive mobile app, the project aims to make waste management accessible and sustainable, contributing to a cleaner and more eco-friendly continent.

Problems

Inefficient waste collection and disposal systems often lead to garbage piling up in communities, causing health hazards and environmental pollution.

The environmental footprint of waste management, including landfill usage and energy consumption, can strain natural resources and contribute to climate change.

Lack of awareness and education about responsible waste management practices results in improper waste disposal and recycling behavior.Many communities lacks.

USER RESEARCH

Research Methods:

Our User research involved a combination of qualitative and quantitative research methods. We conducted surveys and interviews to gather diverse insights, utilizing a mix of open-ended and closed-ended questions. A total of 30 users participated in the research process, providing valuable feedback and perspectives on their expectations from Jadecoapp.

Research Questions:

  1. What would motivated users to download and use JadecoApp?
  2. How do users perceive the goals of JadecoApp on their waste management practices?
  3. What challenges or pain points do users encounter while using other waste management app?
  4. What features of JadecoApp do users find most valuable?
  5. Are there any specific features that users find difficult to use or not effective in other waste management apps?
  6. How do users compare JadecoApp to other waste management apps they have used?
  7. What suggestions do users have for enhancing community engagement features?
  8. How likely are users to continue using JadecoApp in the long term?
